How To Play

Cookie - Continuously click the cookie to create cookies. Increases your cookie count.

Upgrades - Click an upgrade when you have enough cookies. These increase your cookies per second by different amounts.

Cookies Per Second - Determines how many cookies are created automatically for you per second.
